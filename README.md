## Introduction 📚

**FINANSMART** is a comprehensive financial management application designed to help you organize your finances, track your income and expenses, and make informed financial decisions.

## Objectives 🎯

- **Track Finances** 📒: Keep a detailed record of your income and expenses.
- **Visualize Data** 📊: Use charts and tables to understand your financial situation.
- **Receive Recommendations** 💡: Get personalized advice to improve your financial health.

## Functionality ⚙️

- **Add Income and Expenses** 💰: Easily add your financial transactions with predefined categories and descriptions.
- **View Charts** 📈: Visualize your financial data with bar charts and pie charts.
- **View Tables** 📋: See detailed tables of your income and expenses.
- **Get Recommendations** 📝: Receive personalized financial advice based on your data.

## Tools Used 🛠️

- **Streamlit** 🌐: For building the interactive web application.
- **Pandas** 🐼: For data manipulation and analysis.
- **Seaborn** 📊: For creating beautiful visualizations.
- **Matplotlib** 📉: For data visualization.
- **GitHub** 🐙: For version control and collaboration.

## Development Process 🚀

1. **Planning** 🗓️: Define the objectives and functionality of the application.

## Streamlit App 🚀

The **FINANSMART** Streamlit application is designed to provide an intuitive and interactive interface for managing your finances. Here are the key features and how to use them:

### Home Page 🏠

The home page provides an overview of the application and its features. It includes a welcome message and a brief description of what you can do with **FINANSMART**.

### Add Income/Expense 💰

In this section, you can add your income and expenses. Follow these steps:
1. **Select Type**: Choose whether you are adding an income or an expense.
2. **Enter Amount**: Input the amount of money.
3. **Select Category**: Choose a category that best describes the transaction.
4. **Select Description**: Pick a description that provides more details about the transaction.
5. **Add Transaction**: Click the "Add" button to save the transaction.

### View Charts 📊

This section allows you to visualize your financial data through various charts:
- **Bar Charts** 📊: Display your income and expenses categorized by description.
- **Pie Charts** 🥧: Show the distribution of your expenses across different categories.

These visualizations help you understand your financial situation at a glance.

### View Tables 📋

In the "View Tables" section, you can see detailed tables of your income and expenses. This allows for a more granular analysis of your financial data. You can:
- **View Incomes** 💵: See a table of all your income transactions.
- **View Expenses** 💸: See a table of all your expense transactions.

### Recommendations 💡

The recommendations section provides personalized advice based on your financial data. To get recommendations:
1. **Click "Get Recommendations"**: The application will analyze your data and provide tips to help you save more and spend wisely.
